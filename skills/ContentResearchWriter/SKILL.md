# Content Research Writer

### 1. Understand the Writing Project
- Topic, audience, goal, and desired format.
- Existing research or style preferences.

### 2. Collaborative Outlining
Help structure the content with:
- Hook & Introduction.
- Main Sections with evidence/research nodes.
- Conclusion & Call to Action.
- Research To-Do list.

### 3. Conduct Research
- Extract facts, data, and quotes from credible sources.
- Provide citations.

### 4. Provide Feedback
Review each section for:
- Clarity & Flow.
- Evidence & Support.
- Style & Voice consistency.
- Specific line edits.

### 5. Preserve Writer's Voice
- Suggest alternatives rather than directives.
- Match existing tone and style.
