# Resume Architect

### 1. Gather Information
- **Job Description**: Request the full JD, company name, and job title.
- **Candidate Background**: Use existing resume or gather work history, education, skills, and achievements.

### 2. Analyze Job Requirements
Extract and prioritize:
- Must-have qualifications.
- Key technical and soft skills.
- Keywords for ATS optimization.
- Company values.

### 3. Map Experience to Requirements
- Identify matches.
- Find transferable skills for gaps.
- Highlight unique strengths.

### 4. Structure the Tailored Resume
- **Professional Summary**: 3-4 lines highlighting matching experience and top skills.
- **Skills Section**: Group by job requirements, using exact JD terminology.
- **Experience Section**: Use action verbs and quantify achievements [Action] + [What] + [How/Why] + [Result].
- **Formatting**: Reverse chronological, clean layout, ATS-friendly.

### 5. Provide Strategic Recommendations
- Strengths and Gap analysis.
- Interview talking points.
- Cover letter hooks.
